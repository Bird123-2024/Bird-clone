## Table of Contents

The Keep Design System's Avatar component allows you to showcase user avatars or profile images consistently and aesthetically. With customizable choices for placeholders, shapes, status icons, and sizes, you can craft adaptable avatars to suit your design objectives.

## Default Avatar Group

The Avatar Group component facilitates the presentation of user avatars or profile images within a group.
